Advancing the kingdom of God following the New Testament pattern (of the book of acts) in meeting the need of a total man.

ABOUT US:

Profile:

The word ‘CHRISCO’ is an acronym of Christ Co-Workers, (1 Corinthians 3:9) For we are co-workers in God’s service; you are God’s field, God’s building (NIV). This is the reason as to why we exist. Chrisco fellowship of churches Bunamwaya is a fivefold ministry, (Ephesians 4:11) And he gave some, apostles; and some, prophets; and some, evangelists; and some, pastors, and teachers (KJV) For the perfecting of the saints, for the work of the ministry, for the edifying of the body of Christ. We believe that Its God who gives the gifts of Apostle, prophets, evangelist, pastor, and teachers for the perfecting of the saints, for the work of the ministry, for the edifying of the body of Christ. Chrisco Nationality and Internationally. Chrisco fellowship of churches began in Uganda in1983in eastern Uganda. It has spread all over the nation having five presbyteries; Bunamwaya presbytery found in the central region, Jinja and Mbale presbytery found in the eastern Uganda, Kasese presbytery in western Uganda and Bugolobi presbytery in Kampala capital city. These presbyteries are oversighted by a national presbyter/administration who reports to the international center in Newport mews Virginia USA. However, the center for Chrisco churches in Africa is in Nairobi Kenya. In Uganda Chrisco fellowship of churches is affiliated as a full member of the evangelical fellowship of Uganda. Chrisco fellowship Bunamwaya church relates closely with Believers Worship Assembly church Nsangi under their present leader Apostle Dr. Daniel Kibuuka.

Chrisco Vision:

Advancing the kingdom of God following the New Testament pattern (of the book of acts) in meeting the need of a total man.

Chrisco Mission:

To advance the Kingdom of God beginning from our local Bunamwaya community by: Winning souls through sharing the gospel of Jesus Christ (Act 1:8) Discipling through equipping and encouraging every believer to actively engage in works of service (1 corinthians12:7, Ephesians 4:12)

Objectives:

    We have seven-point objectives which guides our operations,
  • To intercede for the nation of Uganda and the world.
  • To win souls to Jesus Christ and plant churches.
  • To disciple new believers until they become workers to disciple others.
  • To affiliate with other churches for fellowship and unity among the body of Christ.
  • To teach and demonstrate the five-fold ministry to help believers come to the unity of the faith.
  • To intercede for and prepare believers for revival.
  • To establish and manage projects to meet the needs of the total man.

Leadership:

The leadership comprises of the presbyter, pastors, elders, deacons, and deaconess. The presbyter is Dr Joshua Ryekokonya and his wife pastor Phyllis Ryekokonya, there are two pastors, Pastor Daniel, and pastor Isaiah Mwalye. there are two elders, four deacons and nine deaconesses.
